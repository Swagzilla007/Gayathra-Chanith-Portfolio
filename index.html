About Me

Hello there! I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT) in Sri Lanka, affiliated with the University of Westminster, UK, specializing in Fullstack development and mobile/desktop application development.

My interest in technology started because I was passionate about solving problems and wanted to make the designs I saw in everyday digital interfaces, apps, and websites better. This enthusiasm eventually developed into a distinct goal: to create dependable, approachable software with slick, contemporary UI/UX elements that appeal to consumers.

Beyond academics, I enjoy playing football and exploring fragrances. I love listening to Pop, rap music, and EDM, which keeps me motivated and upbeat. These diverse interests frequently inspire concepts for innovative design and user-centered elements in my work.
